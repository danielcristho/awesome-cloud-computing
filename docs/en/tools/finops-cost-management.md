## FinOps Principles

> Common principles used in cloud financial operations practices.

### Cultural Aspects

- **Shared responsibility** - Collaboration between engineering, finance, and business
- **Business value focus** - Cost decisions aligned with business outcomes
- **Continuous improvement** - Ongoing optimization process
- **Data-driven decisions** - Decisions based on usage and cost metrics

### Core Practices

- **Cost visibility** - Access to timely and accurate cost data
- **Allocation and chargeback** - Attribution of costs to teams or workloads
- **Optimization** - Identifying and reducing unnecessary spend
- **Governance** - Policies and controls for managing cloud usage
